About Me

Welcome to my professional portfolio! I'm a sophomore at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, pursuing a B.S. in Industrial Engineering with a track in Economics and Finance. With a strong academic record (GPA: 3.89), I'm passionate about leveraging technology and data-driven insights to solve complex problems.

As the Founder and CEO of BlockTrace, I'm leading a blockchain startup focused on digital asset tracking and security. My work involves developing decentralized solutions that enhance transparency and prevent fraud in the digital economy. This entrepreneurial journey has taught me valuable lessons about innovation, leadership, and the intersection of technology and business.

My research experience spans cutting-edge areas including EEG-based decision-making analysis and machine learning applications in financial engineering. I've worked with advanced technologies including neural signal processing, connectivity analysis, and predictive modeling for European options pricing. This combination of technical expertise and business acumen positions me uniquely at the intersection of engineering, finance, and entrepreneurship.

Beyond academics and entrepreneurship, I'm actively involved in campus organizations including Zero2One startup incubator, NOBE (National Organization for Business and Engineering) as a Business Consultant, Financial Engineering Club, and Macro Investment Association as a Junior Analyst. I also serve as an Engineering Student Admissions Representative, helping prospective students discover opportunities at Illinois.
